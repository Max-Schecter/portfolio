PROVE - Charging Port

Design Requirements:

  • Needed to seamlessly mate with the vehicle’s organic geometry.
  • Must house a J1772 charging plug.
  • Must be easy to manufacture.

Method:

  • I selected the back left of the vehicle due to access, proximity to high-voltage components, and available space.
  • After looking at pre exisitng designs, I chose a hinged design and decided to make a bespoke part instead of buying becasue of the unique vehicle geometry.
  • I then modeled the port in Siemens NX, designing the part with the intention to 3d print it.

Results:

  • I 3d printed a number of prototypes and confirmed manufacturability and functionality.
  • I'm waiting on the completion of the vehicle Aeroshell to integrate it and perform final tests.
