PROVE - Headlights and Tail Lights

Design Requirements:

  • Need to interface with the orgainc geometry of the vehicle.
  • The headlight module must be able to house a five inch deep projector headlight and fit within tight geometry constraints.
  • The tail light bar must act both as a structural member in addition to its visual function.

Design Requirements:

  • Need to interface with the orgainc geometry of the vehicle.
  • The headlight module must be able to house a five inch deep projector headlight and fit within tight geometry constraints.
  • The tail light bar must act both as a structural member in addition to its visual function.

Method:

    I used Siemens NX for modeling and Blender to visualize the final designs.
  • The headlight and tail light cover is going to be manufactured using acrylic vaccum forming.
  • The housings are going to be 3d printed to due the bespoke nature of the proejct.

Results:

  • I am in the process of prototyping the headlight and tail lightmodule, with an aim to integrate it into the vehicle next year.
  • After careful consideration, we decided to scrap the tail light bar, instead opting for the two side light elements and a purelty strucutral member running accross the lenght of the vehilce.
