Projects

PROVE - Headlights and Tail Lights

Description:
  • Designing and prototyping the headlights and tail lights for the Cal Poly PROVE EV.
  • Using Siemens NX for modeling and Blender to visualize final designs.
  • Final model printed in PETG with a UV‐resistant resin for the diffuser.

PROVE - Charging Port

Designed and manufactured a charge port housing for the Cal Poly PROVE electric vehicle.

PLA prototypes were created before finalizing a PETG print for durability and strength.

Replacement Part for Craftsman Jointer Planer

Used Fusion 360 to redesign and 3D print a replacement fence trolley for a 1980s jointer-planer.

Printed using PLA for its low cost and sustainability.

PROVE - Aeroshell Redesign

Analyzed and redesigned the carbon fiber aeroshell for the Cal Poly PROVE electric vehicle.

  • Verified panel geometry for layup.
  • Created a precise alignment system for the aeroshell.
  • Designed a cooling air duct and analyzed tolerances for wheel motion.

Popcorn Popper Fan Assembly
